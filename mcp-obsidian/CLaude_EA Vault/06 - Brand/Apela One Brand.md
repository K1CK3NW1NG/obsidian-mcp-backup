# Apela One — Brand & Visual Identity

← [[../WORK]] | [[../ME]]


**Tagline:** Powering Growth on Autopilot.

## Logo
- Primary logo: circular "A" mark with logotype (use SVG when available)
- Clearspace: Maintain at least the height of the circular mark on all sides
- Never stretch, recolor, or rotate the logo

## Color Palette
- Primary: `#FF5A1F` (orange)
- Accent/dark: `#E24A17`
- Background dark: `#0F0F10`
- Neutral darks: `#3B2A24`, `#2A2A2A`
- Gray: `#8A8A8A`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography
- Primary UI / web: DM Sans
- Display / logo: Manison

## Mood & Imagery
- Warm, energetic, modern — gradients blending deep charcoal to vibrant orange
- Use product screenshots, hands-on-device shots, and simple motion graphics

## Brand Voice
- Internal: Casual + professional + enthusiastic (short bullet summaries)
- External: Professional, clear, benefits-first
- Emojis: Never unless explicitly requested
